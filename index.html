The Tricolour and Our History

India's tricolour flag, with its saffron, white, and green stripes, is a symbol of our nation's rich history and diverse culture. Saffron represents courage and sacrifice, white stands for peace and truth, and green symbolizes faith and prosperity. The Ashoka Chakra in the center signifies the wheel of law and righteousness.
